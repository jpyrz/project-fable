# Naming directions

These are creative territories, not trademark or domain-cleared finalists. Keep the game name, world name, and companion category separate so one choice does not have to do every job.

## Cozy place-led

- **Bramblewick** — game/world; companions could be called **Kin**.
- **Starmeadow** — game/world; companions could be **Wanderlings**.
- **Hearthwild** — game/world; companions could be **Wildkin**.
- **Tanglemoon** — game/world; companions could be **Moonlings**.
- **Wispwood** — game/world; companions could be **Wisps** or **Kin**.

## Companion-led

- **Wonderkin** — companions are **Kin**.
- **Bloomkin** — companions are **Bloomkin**.
- **Whimlings** — companions are **Whimlings**.
- **Hearthlings** — companions are **Hearthlings**.
- **Spritelings** — companions are **Sprites** or **Spritelings**.

## Storybook pairings

- **Moss & Moon**
- **Pebble & Bloom**
- **The Cozy Wilds**
- **Little Lanterns**
- **Wildwish**

## Current recommendation

Keep **Bramblewick** as the world name for now and use the neutral word **companion** in interface copy. Explore **Wildwish**, **Wonderkin**, and **The Cozy Wilds** as game-name territories later. “Kin” is the strongest short category word, but it should be tested in sentences and cleared before adoption.
